A word from the Founder

Hi I’m Al, a front end web designer / developer and founder of ahha web solutions.

Based in Barnsley, South Yorkshire – my bread and butter is made from crafting front ends and interfaces for websites that work regardless of screensize or device.

The web is a powerful medium with multiple disciplines. I specialise in the parts that make your website look good, load fast and are accessible on whatever device or browser your target audience wishes to use.

Although my main interest is front end development, I started out in graphic design way back in the early 90s and transitioned from this to web site design and build at the dawn of the internet. I like to think my technical knowledge and design awareness make a good combination to help deliver attractive, usable and effective websites.

I use Wordpress as the backend to manage the content of your site.
