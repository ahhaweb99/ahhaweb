Jigsaw Communication App
Wordpress/Javascript Rest API
website/app build

I was approached by Script Media to rescue a project that had been promised by another developer but they had been let down. The work involved creating a learning app called Jigsaw Communication which is a 3 Stage, early intervention programme that helps support pupils with their language acquisition. Teachers register and login to the app, and add their own students, who then partake in a series of visual questions relating to observations of the various Jigsaw pieces. Progress is saved in the CMS and users can pick up their progress at a later date.
